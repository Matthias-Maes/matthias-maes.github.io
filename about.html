About MM-13

Who Am I

I am MM-13. I am a student at the Digital Arts & Entertainment University of Belgium. I am passionate about making games and exploring new worlds. I love being creative and giving others the opportunities to be creative and do what they enjoy.

My Goals

My goals are to start my own game studio and make fun to play games for both you and your friends. I want to share my passion for games with the community and enjoy making games.

What I Like

I like to make games that are fun and easy to play, but I am passionate enough to get into bigger projects and explore higher levels of the gaming industry. I like seeing people do something they love and I think that those people working together, can make the best games you'll play.
